# Data Sources

This document tracks the sources used to compile the Saskatoon Art Guide catalog.

## Source Types

- **Artist submission**: Information provided directly by the artist who created the work
- **Public record**: Information from municipal records, press releases, or official documentation
- **Community contribution**: Information submitted by community members or observers
- **Archival research**: Information gathered from historical records or archives
- **Site documentation**: Information collected through direct observation and photography

## Source Validation

All entries are reviewed for accuracy before inclusion. When possible, information is cross-referenced with:

- Artist websites and portfolios
- Municipal arts databases
- News articles and press releases
- Commissioning organization records

## Attribution Priority

In cases where sources conflict, priority is given to:

1. Direct artist confirmation
2. Commissioning organization records
3. Contemporary documentation (photos, articles from time of creation)
4. Community submissions with verifiable details

## Ongoing Maintenance

Sources are reviewed periodically to ensure:

- Links to artist websites remain current
- Contact information is up to date
- Historical records are preserved even if physical artworks are removed
